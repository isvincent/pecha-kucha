📖 Show vs. Tell 範例示範
❌ Tell — 說明
「便利商店很方便。」"The convenience store is so convenient."
✅ Show — 展示
「走出門不到兩百步，就能買到熱騰騰的包子跟繳掉罰單。」"Less than two hundred steps from your door: steaming buns, and a place to pay your fine."

「考試讓我壓力很大。」"Exams make me really stressed."
✅ Show — 展示
「凌晨三點，盯著天花板，手機靜音，但忍不住一直解鎖又鎖回去。」"3 a.m. Staring at the ceiling. Phone on silent — but I keep unlocking it and locking it again."

ACT 6

🔊 重音放大鏡The Stress Magnifier

10 min▼
🎙 Teacher Instruction 教師指令
將下方台詞投影給全班。第一次：平音齊讀。第二次：把標色的形容詞和動詞音量加倍、語速放慢。請學生互評感受到的差異。
Round 1: Read together in a flat, even tone. Round 2: Double the volume on highlighted adjectives and verbs, and slow down. Ask students to notice the difference.
📋 你的任務 Your Task
第一次平讀，第二次把標色的詞說得更大聲、更慢，感受聲音的力量！First, read it flat. Second time, make highlighted words louder and slower. Feel the power!
■ 形容詞 Adjective → 放大聲 Louder ■ 動詞 Verb → 放慢說 Slower （點擊單字切換強調 / Click a word to toggle）
🔑 技巧提示 Pro Tip
重音不只是「大聲」——是在關鍵詞前微微停頓，然後加重、放慢。觀眾的耳朵會自動抓住那個詞。Stress isn't just volume — it's a tiny pause before the key word, then heavier, slower delivery. The audience's ears will lock onto it automatically.
